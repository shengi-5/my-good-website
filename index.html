Zayed's Cinematic Journal

A space where I express my thoughts on the shows and manga that left a mark on me.

The Truman Show

This movie was absolute cinema in every sense of the word. It keeps you hooked from start to finish—even the shortest attention span wouldn't stand a chance against the grip of this story.

Chainsaw Man: Reze Arc

Words cannot fully describe the beauty of this arc. It left me with a unique feeling—one that I still find hard to articulate to this day. It's a hauntingly beautiful experience.

This story is definitely going to stick in my memory for a long time...

Goodnight Punpun

Goodnight Punpun is the most intense manga I have ever read. I actually stopped reading manga for a while after finishing this because the emotional impact was so raw and unexpected—especially from a story where the main character is drawn as a simple, silly little bird.

I hope Punpun finally finds peace. He has certainly suffered enough.
